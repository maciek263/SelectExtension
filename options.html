Help:

Press Shift+Ctrl+S (Shift+CMD+S on Mac) to pop up a text input field to let you quickly search on your favorite search engine.

The default is DuckDuckGo, you can select some other defaults if you want to, but keep in mind, that you can cover most of your needs with DuckDuckGo's !bang Syntax.

BETA

Please note, that this plugin is in beta state, while it is usable, it does not yet provide all planned functionality.

Useful DuckDuckGo !bang examples

  • !w term - Search on wikipedia
  • !wde term - Search on german wikipedia
  • !leo word - Translate a word
  • !g term - Search on google (anonymized through duckduckgo)
  • 10 USD in EUR - An example on how to use the currency calculator
